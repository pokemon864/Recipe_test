My German Chocolate Dump Cake Recipe

Description

A cake where the batter,filling and topping elements are all baked together.It has a rich, moist texture, a delicious chocolate and coconut flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 cups toasted pecans, chopped, divided
  • 1 cup sweetened flaked coconut
  • 1 (2-layer size) package devil's food cake mix
  • eggs, oild, and water as specified for the cake mix
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2/3 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine 1 cup of the pecans and the coconut. Spread the mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  3. In a large bowl, prepare the cake mix according to package instructions. Pour the batter over the pecan and coconut mixture in the baking dish.
  4. In a medium bowl, beat together the cream cheese, melted butter, vanilla extract, powdered sugar, and remaining 2/3 cup of pecans until smooth. Drop spoonfuls of the cream cheese mixture over the cake batter in the baking dish. Sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow to cool before serving.
