My Shoyu Chicken Recipe

Description

Shoyu Chicken is a popular Chinese dish that features tender chicken pieces cooked in a savory soy sauce-based marinade. The chicken is typically marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and other seasonings, then simmered until it becomes flavorful and succulent. This dish is often served with steamed rice and vegetables, making it a delicious and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in a mixture of soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Remove the chicken from the marinade and cook until golden brown on all sides.
  4. Pour in the water and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
